3. Connecting with Your Community

Your Nanodegree community will play a huge role in supporting you when you get stuck and in helping you deepen your learning. Getting to know your fellow students will also make your experience a lot more fun!

To ask and answer questions and contribute to discussions, head to the forum. You can also get there from any Classroom page using the forum link on the left.

In the forum, the Udacity Coach team, as well as the other students in your cohort, will be there to help out. You can search to see if someone has already asked a question related to yours, or you can make a new post if no one has.
